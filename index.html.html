Sat 25San Lorenzo → Noto at golden hour

BeachCulture

San Lorenzo beach

Turquoise water and fine sand through the hot hours. It's Saturday — book lido sunbeds ahead, free-beach stretches vanish fast.

17:30

Noto baroque walk

Cathedral (open to 20:00), Via Nicolaci, Palazzo Nicolaci — the limestone glows at this hour. Aperitivo and dinner on the corso.

☀

Why this order: Noto at July noon is a white furnace; at golden hour it's the best light of the day, minus the heat and the tour groups.

Sun 26Syracuse and Ortigia

Culture

morningmiddayevening

9:00

Neapolis archaeological park

Greek theatre and the Ear of Dionysius at opening. In July the cavea is partly rigged for the play season — the evening shows are the real event (see Music below).

11:30

Ortigia

Duomo, market lunch (Caseificio Borderi — expect a line), then a midday swim off the Forte Vigliena rocks. Sunset walk on the seafront, dinner in the lanes.

☀

Sunday logic: beaches are at weekend peak — a city with a rocky swim spot beats fighting for sand today.

Mon 27Calamosche and Marianelli

Beach

morningmiddayevening

8:00

Calamosche at opening

The reserve's iconic cove, half empty on a Monday morning. 15–20 min walk from the lot on a rough track; bring lunch and water — there's nothing at the beach.

14:00

Marianelli

Calamosche's wilder twin, reachable on foot along the coast. Dunes, clear water, no services.

19:00

Marzamemi

Aperitivo and dinner in Piazza Regina Margherita — the fishing village at its liveliest on a summer evening. Book a table.

⚠

Two honest notes: Marianelli is the area's historic naturist beach (mixed crowd, no obligation). And leave nothing in the car — break-ins reported at the dirt parking lots.

Tue 28Cavagrande canyon

Nature

morningmiddayevening

8:00

Scala Cruci descent

~1h steep hike down into the canyon, swim in the chain of natural pools and small waterfalls, climb back out before noon.

Afternoon

Full rest

Shower, nap, pool. Quiet dinner in Noto. No beach today — the climb out is a workout and the legs earned it.

⛰

Non-negotiables: real sneakers (the trail wrecks sandals) and 2 litres of water each. If a strong scirocco is forecast for Wednesday, swap this day with the beaches.

Wed 29Isola delle Correnti and Carratois

Beach

morningmiddayevening

9:00

Isola delle Correnti

Sicily's southernmost tip, where the Ionian and the Mediterranean visibly meet. Quiet on a Wednesday; the swim between two seas is the photo of the trip.

13:00

Carratois

Five minutes up the coast: Caribbean-white sand, seafood lunch with feet in the sand at a lido, lazy afternoon under an umbrella.

🌬

Wind check: these south-facing beaches turn rough and murky under scirocco. If it blows, swap with the Cavagrande day — the canyon doesn't care about wind.
